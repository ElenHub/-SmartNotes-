What is SmartNotes?
SmartNotes is an innovative application that allows you to flexibly combine notes with tasks.
Moreover, SmartNotes allows you to attach photos or voice recordings to each note or task
to help you organize your life. SmartNotes uses a new innovative technology that allows you
to group your notes
How do I get started with SmartNotes?
Getting started with SmartNotes is easy! Simply visit our website, sign up for a free trial, and
start exploring the features. Our intuitive interface and comprehensive onboarding materials
will guide you through the process.
What are the key features of SmartNotes?
Features include simple mathematical input, instant code execution in the cloud, Wolfram
